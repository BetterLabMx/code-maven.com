<h3>Flag of Switzerland</h3>

<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flag_of_Switzerland">wikipedia</a> and
<a href="http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Flag_of_Switzerland.svg">wikimedia svg</a>.

According to <a href="http://www.bk.admin.ch/themen/02268/02385/index.html?lang=de">CD Bund V7.0</a>
the red colour of the flags is #FF0000, the with of the
middle width = 1/5 of the full width
side   width = 1/5 of the full width + 1/6 of the middle width (= 0.2 + 0.2 / 6 = 1.4/6)
